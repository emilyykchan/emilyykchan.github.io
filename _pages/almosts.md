<summary><strong>[Apr 2026] BJSM manuscript rejection</strong></summary>

<br>

<strong>What happened:</strong><br>
A manuscript was desk rejected from the <em>British Journal of Sports Medicine</em> because it was not considered to have sufficient clinical relevance for the journal.
For a biomechanics-heavy manuscript, it is somewhat expected - but not really, because BJSM does look for papers that potentially influence guidelines, and we have made this clear in the manuscript. 

<br><br>

<strong>Next step: </strong><br>
Identify more suitable journal and revise for that.
